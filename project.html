"सहायम्" (Sahayam)

The project is titled "सहायम्" (Sahayam), which translates to "Help" in English. It is a donation platform dedicated to providing nutritious food to those in need. The platform offers various donation options, allowing contributors to support initiatives like feeding individuals during Makar Sankranti and pilgrims during Kumbh Mela. Donors can choose predefined amounts or specify custom contributions to support these causes. The mission of सहायम् is to combat hunger by facilitating community-driven support and ensuring that nutritious meals reach the underprivileged.
